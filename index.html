About Juliet

Juliet Shen is a user advocate with 3+ years experience in startup environments. She currently resides in Los Angeles, CA with her partner and two cats. After spending the beginning of her career working in politics and nonprofits, Juliet made the jump to the tech industry to join some of the best and brightest at Karma WiFi. In October of 2016, Tinder managed to lure her away from New York and to the west coast.

She's currently studying full-stack web development at UCLA Extension under the tutelage of Cameron Manavian while she continues to work at Tinder. Passionate about user experience, she is the first to volunteer and the last to leave the office.
